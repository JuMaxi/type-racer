How to Take the Typing Test
  1. Select a difficulty level from the dropdown menu (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
  2. Click inside the text area to start typing the displayed text.
  3. Type the text as accurately and quickly as possible.
  4. Press the "Enter" key to finish the test.
  5. View your results, including Words Per Minute (WPM) and time taken.
  6. Click the "Retry" button to try again at the same difficulty level.
